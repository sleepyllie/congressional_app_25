Our Mission

We strive to promote equity between the population of people who have trouble with their meals -whether it be allergy or otherwise related- and those who have no issues in their everyday lives.

Our Story

I, the creator of this application, have struggled with rare food allergies as well as stomach issues, and as a result, finding grocery products or meals that I've been able to consume safely. Many of my friends have also suffered from food allergies and autoimmune diseases like Celiac disease.
